### FAQ Section

1. **Is ProTrader5 suitable for beginners?**
   Yes! Our platform offers educational resources, demo accounts, and copy trading to help beginners learn from experienced traders.

2. **Which exchanges are supported?**
   We support NSE, BSE, MCX, NCDEX (India), Binance (crypto), and more exchanges being added regularly.

3. **How secure is my money?**
   We use bank-grade encryption, two-factor authentication, and never store your trading passwords. Your funds remain in your exchange accounts.

4. **Can I try before I buy?**
   Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.

5. **What's the minimum deposit?**
   There's no minimum deposit for the platform. However, exchanges may have their own requirements (typically $100-$500).

6. **Do you offer customer support?**
   Yes! Email support for all users, live chat for Professional tier, and dedicated account manager for Enterprise.

7. **Can I cancel anytime?**
   Yes, cancel anytime. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

8. **Is there a mobile app?**
   Yes! iOS and Android apps with full feature parity to the web platform.

9. **How does copy trading work?**
   Browse top traders, select strategies to follow, set your investment amount, and trades are automatically copied to your account.

10. **What payment methods do you accept?**
    Credit/debit cards, bank transfers, UPI, PayPal, and cryptocurrency.
